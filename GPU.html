Video Card

The video card, also known as the graphics card, is an expansion card used to generate a feed of output images to a display. They are often advertised as dedicated graphics cards, as opposed to the integrated graphics in the CPU. In both the intergrated graphics and the video card the core of the output is the GPU (the graphics porcessing unit). Video cards can be used for gaming, in workstations, Cryptocurrency mining and etc.
