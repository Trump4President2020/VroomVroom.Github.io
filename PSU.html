What Does PSU Stand For?

PSU stands for Power Supply Unit.

What does the Power Supply Unit do?

A PSU converts main AC to low-voltage DC power for the computer internals.
it essentially powers the computer. Some power supplies have a manual switch for
selecting input voltage, while others automatically adapt to the mains voltage.
